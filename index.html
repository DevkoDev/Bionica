Bionica

Bionica is a collection of 8,000 NFTs (non-fungible tokens) which stand for advocacy and action in bringing social change.

Clutching the fist of their Bionic arm triumphantly in the air, the Bionicas are a shining symbol of hope and conviction.

We envision Bionica as a community across borders who believe in advocacy, awareness, and action in supporting important social causes.

Vision

Our aim is to bring purposeful social impact into the NFT space, utilising the power of blockchain in contributing to social good.

We are committed to bringing actionable through the transparency of blockchain, and the role NFTs can play building a community with shared values and goals.

We want to create a bridge where our message of doing good can be shared on the Metaverse, and make an impact in the real world through the Bionica Community-Led Fund.

Origin

“That’s so cool”, a phrase that made a 8-year-old amputee feel like her disability was a super power. She was fitted with an Iron Man inspired prosthetic arm. She felt invincible.

Little did one man know that an Iron Man prosthetic arm he designed, and 3D-printed at home could mean the world to this child.

Bionica is inspired by the action a single person can take to make life better for others.

FAQ

What is an NFT?
An NFT is a “non-fungible token” which is a unique token created, stored, and encrypted on a blockchain. This means that an NFT cannot be duplicated, making it one-of-a-kind.
How can i buy Bionica NFT
Ensure that you have a MetaMask wallet (or set one up if you don’t!). If you have a MetaMask wallet ready, ensure that there is enough ETH in your Meta Mask wallet to cover the mint cost and gas fees.
What is MetaMask?
Creating a MetaMask (digital wallet) means getting an Ethereum address where you can store cryptocurrencies required to purchase your NFT, and store your NFTs after minting them.
What is the supply?
There will be a total supply of 8,000 unique Bionica NFTs.
When is the launch date?
Bionica NFT is planned for launch in 2022 Q2.
